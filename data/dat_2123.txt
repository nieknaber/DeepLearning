As a general rule, Match 3 games are easy, repetitive, and can quickly become boring.  Jewel Quest Heritage is not one of those.  The authors have taken Match 3 to a greater level of difficulty, requiring the player to look ahead and plan, logically, their next move(s).<br /><br />The graphics in this game are beautiful, and the game itself is challenging.  About the only weak area is the story line, but Match 3 players are probably not very interested in the story line.<br /><br />The game's the thing -- and this one has many challenges to be enjoyed.<br /><br />By the way, Amazon's version comes with an extra Jewel Quest game.  If you are unfamiliar with Jewel Quest, the additional game is a great place to start getting acquainted.
